About Porthtowan Surfshop and Coffee Bar

Porthtowan Surfshop and Coffee Bar provides surfing lessons and equipment hire all year round on Porthtowan Beach, Cornwall. We also have a bar serving fresh coffee and soft drinks to cap the end to your perfect day on the waves.

Surf School

We are one of the most private schools on the island of Tenerife. Since not only we pretend that you learn to surf, but also show you the wonderful world that surrounds this sport.

FRANZ SURF SCHOOL in Tenerife offers surf lessons for all ages and surf level! Doesn't matters if you are a kid or and adult, without experience or a great surfer! With us you will find your surf class. We have the surfboard that you need, for different levels and conditions and also the individual advice for more learning. The lessons, taught by professional instructors, are two hours (15 minutes of theory and 1 hour and 45 minutes of training in water) and are given in English, Spanish, Italian and Russian, according to your preference.
